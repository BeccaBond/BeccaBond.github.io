Scientific Eco-Monitoring Rover, "Sonic"

Summary

I built Sonic with a classmate to create a robust rover platform capable of environmental monitoring, sensor testing and remote scientific support. The rover was designed to serve as a terrestrial testing platform for Olin College's CREST (Climate Robotics and Expeditionary Science Technology) lab and related underwater robotics research by enabling reliable field testing of sensors and embedded systems before their deployment in aquatic environments.

Along with integrating a ruggedized mechanical design, working on Sonic was my deep-dive into the world of software engineering. Check out our research paper and learnings below! ↓
